Every route change now runs through a shared fade-and-lift transition, so moving between Home, About, Projects, and Blog feels like one continuous surface instead of hard page swaps.

- One `PageTransition` wrapper at the root
- `AnimatePresence` handles enter/exit
- Also plays on first paint for a softer landing
